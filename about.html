Who we are?


Tours Operators Esperanza is a tour operator company receptive / emissive that is responsible for showing the charms of the Republic Dominican; both our beaches, as well as our culture and our ecology. We develop Sustainable Tourism for the benefit of the community, while we take care of the ecology, and this valuable culture that we have full of color, charm, fun and flavor gastronomic. Our ancestral tradition leaves tourists who visit us fascinated with these yearning to know it thoroughly.

WHO WE ARE

Tours operadores esperanza vacation Tour and Travel is a DMC, Destination Management Company, a receptive tour operator in the Dominican Republic. We are all you need in the Caribbean destination. Colonial Tours is a reliable company with more than 10 years of experience and committed to the highest level of service and professionalism, constantly researching and applying new technology to create a seamless reservation process, speed of response and hospitality. We are based in Santo Domingo with branch offices in the most important tourism zones of the Dominican Republic: Punta Cana, La Romana, Samana, Boca Chica, Puerto Plata.
